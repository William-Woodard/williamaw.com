About me

Rower

I haven been rowing competively since I was 12 and reguarly compete in national events. As a sculler I came 7th in the country in British Junior Championships in 2018 and 13th in 2019. In 2021 I came 9th in the eights category and then went on to win blades in the first division of Cambridge town bumps. I have recently been elected Hills Road Boys captain and Cantabs Junior Cantabs.

Coder

I taught myself python using a Raspberry Pi given to me for my 10th birthday. Whilst still mainly using Python, I have learnt lots of C++ through coding in the Arduino IDE as well as HTML and JavaScript while coding this website.

Designer

My passion is designing products and coming up with new ideas. I enjoy taking part in hackathons with the combination of product and design it. I have extensive experience with Fusion 360 for CAD and KiCad for PCB design.

Maker

This is the process I enjoy the most. I have built my own 3d printer and love upgrading and fixing it. I find the best part of projects is the satisfaction of combining different parts/materials to make the product.
